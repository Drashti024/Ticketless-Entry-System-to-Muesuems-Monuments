About Surat Fort

The Surat castle is one of the ancient monuments of 16th century existing in the city and bears a significant relevance to its history. However, such a great fortification built to provide the citizens of Surat with an adequate defence against the attacks of the invaders seems to have been forgotten from the minds of the present generation.

How to Reach There

 By Bus:  Looking for the nearest stop or station to Surat Fort?Its within the City and 3 Kms from the Surat Central Bus Stand

 By Train:  Nearest Railway Station To Surat Fort is Surat Station which is situated at a distance of 3km.

 By Plane:  Nearest airport to Surat Fort is Surat Airport which is situated at a distance of 13km. You can book a cab from here.
