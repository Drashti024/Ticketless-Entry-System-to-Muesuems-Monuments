Popular cities

Ahmedabad


Ahmedabad, in western India, is the largest city in the state of Gujarat. One of the fastest growing cities in India, Ahmedabad is a centre for information technology, education and industries.Uttarayan and Navratri are two festivals that are celebrated with great zeal and gusto.

Surat


Surat is a city located on the western part of India in the state of Gujarat.Surat is one of the cleanest city of India and is also known by several other names like "THE SILK CITY", "THE DIAMOND CITY", "THE GREEN CITY", etc. It has the most vibrant present and an equally varied heritage of the past

Vadodara


Vadodara also known as Baroda, is the second largest city in the Indian state of Gujarat.The city is named for its abundance of the Banyan (Vad) tree. Vadodara is also locally referred to as the Sanskari Nagari and Kala Nagari of India.It is situated on the banks of the Vishwamitri River,

Kutch


Kutch district is a district of Gujarat state in western India, with its headquarters at Bhuj.Kutch literally means something which intermittently becomes wet and dry

 

Junagadh


Junagadh district is located in western Gujarat and is surrounded by Arabian sea and forest area.Located at the foot of the Girnar hills, 355 kilometres (221 mi) southwest of Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar (the state capital), it is the seventh largest city in the state.

 

Somnath

Somnath is a magnificent temple situated in Sagar Kant of Saurashtra in Gujarat state. One of the 12 holy Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva is in Jyotirlinga here in Somnath. Somnath is also mentioned in Rigveda
