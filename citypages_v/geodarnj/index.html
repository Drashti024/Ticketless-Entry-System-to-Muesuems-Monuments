BHAVNATH FAIR

The five-day festival at Bhavnath Temple near Damodar Kund is held during Mahashivaratri (February-March). The festival is celebrated with great fanfare and many devotees do a ‘parikrama’ around the Girnar Mountains covering a total distance of 7 km.
